NEW ALBUM

The dynamic duo of Machine Gun Kelly and Travis Barker are back at it again on another album. The rapper and the blink-182 drummer announced Monday (Aug. 9) that they would team up once more on Born With Horns, MGK’s sixth album, by showing off their matching tattoos of the album title on their arms. Barker will once again be credited as the executive producer of the album.
